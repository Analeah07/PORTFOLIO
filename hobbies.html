INSTRUMENTS

Music expresses that which cannot be said and on which it is impossible to be silent. Being an introvert, music become my hobby since I was small. Listening to music literally increase my fighting spirit, and this two instrument: the keyboard and guitar are the instrument that I play here in our chapel. I am a part of music team here in our church. And this is my humbly way to give back the talent that God has given me.

CANVA EDITING

As pandemic arise in the world, I thought of a hobby that I can do while I'm home and someone told me that I can edit designs on canva. I love taking pictures and editing pictures. I'm not an expert right now but I hope someday I will be good to it.

PLAYING WITH DOG

A dog is the only thing on earth that loves you more than he loves himself. When I was little, I didn't see the value of dogs until I had one. The day won't past that I don't see my dog, I love them because they are protective when it comes to their owner, even though they can't speak but you can see on their eyes that they listen and feel what you're going through. They ease the stress and pain that I experience.
